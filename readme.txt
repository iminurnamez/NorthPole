North Pole Tycoon: Old Timey-Toys Division

Times have changed and a hoop and stick doesn't impart the same joy it once did.
The Old-Timey Toy Division of Santa's Workshop has been shuttered to make room
for the rapidly growing Things That Start With i Department. It's up to you to keep
the old-timey traditions alive by proving the Old-Timey Toys Division can pull its 
own weight by building a new operation from scratch.
